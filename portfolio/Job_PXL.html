Environment/Context

Looking for a way to earn some pocket money, my mother offered me to work for Christmas doing gift wrapping in her Ici Paris XL store in Waterloo. It was my first student job and I would continue to do gift wrapping every Christmas for 5 years.

What I've learned and how will it serves me

Apart from having gained skills in gift wrapping, which has served me more than once, I also gained more confidence and contact with customers.
I had to deal with many different client profiles which allowed me to test my adaptability and above all my patience. I have also learned a lot from these different customers, whether it be :

  • The "in a rush" and rude customer where i needed to focus on to get clean packaging as quickly as possible while remaining courteous and withstanding pressure
  • The friendly client fascinated by our skills and with whom we can linger a bit and have fun experimenting with more complex techniques.
I think the fact that I'm able to withstand pressure come from here and I'm pretty sure it will come in handy in my day-to-day life as well as in my work.

Conclusion

I am happy to have started quite young in the world of work, it allowed me to learn some fundamentals which are still useful to me today.
