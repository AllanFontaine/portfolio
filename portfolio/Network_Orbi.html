Environment/Context

My father was complaining about having more and more problem with our home network wifi. Since we only had the box of the ISP, the box was strugling to play both the role of the modem and the router. Knowing that, I've taken the decision to buy an external router to ease the work of the box and also to add the possibility to have access to better connexion when ISP will provide better internet access.

What I've learned and how will it serves me

Actually at my surprise I've learned a lot more than I expected. There is a lot of technologies into modern router that ensure simplicity of uses, modularity, better coverage, QOS management and access to more complex technologies. I've learned about topic such as beamforming, tri-band/dual-band router, router mesh, support of wifi-6, QOS management, ...
Since I want to work in the cybersecurity field, I think that a good sense of what are the technologies currently available on the market is a plus.

Conclusion

I've a way better wifi now and a better view of what the technologies related to it consist of!
